Ciao Vino

Ciao Vino wine bar is the perfect combination of a warm and welcoming place, with great service and competent staff, who will bring to you the classics of our cuisine and a wine list with international labels.

Ciao Vino is rooted in the history of wine with the knowledge, taste, and passion to branch out and experiment.
