Our Story

Antonio Race

Antonio Race was born in a small town on the coast, north of Naples, called Miliscola.

Giuseppe Race has been a fisherman, from a long line of fishermen, and farmer all his life. His mother, Assunta, has been a 'traditional' housewife. Assunta has been blessed with a great gift for culinary arts. Giuseppe and Assunta have five beautiful children (not so young today): Salvatore, Vincenzo, Antonio, Filippo, and Mara. Though they all possess a fondness for cuisine, Antonio is the only one to pursue this love, and make it his livelihood.

Antonio learned from an early age about the joys of culinary expression. His ancestors have been suppliers of fresh sea food for generations. For centuries, the family has grown their own produce, made their own wines, breads, and gourmet cuisine.
