Events

Here at Ciao Vino we organize a number of events: cultural talks, meetings on the arts of good food, wine tastings with the presence of the winemakers, short introductory courses or in-depth incursions on specific topics related to food and wine.

Throughout the year, the wine bar hosts art exhibitions related to the themes of food, natural environments and beyond. In collaboration with our business partners you will find a small library showcasing books on wine, cooking, environmental and sustainability issues.

Stay up to date on all the events of Ciao Vino by visiting our website or following us on Facebook and Instagram.
